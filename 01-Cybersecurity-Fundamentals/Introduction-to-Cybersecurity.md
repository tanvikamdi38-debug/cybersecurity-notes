# 🛡️ Introduction to Cybersecurity

## What is Cybersecurity?
The name itself explains its meaning:
**Cyber + Security = Protecting digital systems from cyber threats.**

Cybersecurity is all about securing our systems, networks, devices, and data from cyber attacks and unauthorized access.
The primary goal of cybersecurity is to protect our information and systems from attackers while maintaining the security of an organization or an individual.

---

## Why is Cybersecurity Important?
As technology continues to grow, cyber attacks are also increasing. Today, almost everyone relies on digital technology, whether it is an individual or an organization.
Cybersecurity helps protect:

- Personal information
- Financial data
- Business operations
- Government systems
- Critical infrastructure

Without proper cybersecurity, attackers can steal sensitive information, disrupt services, or cause financial and reputational damage.
A simple real-life example is locking the door of your house. If you leave your door unlocked, anyone can enter your house. Similarly, if you don't secure your systems and data, attackers may gain unauthorized access.
Cybersecurity acts as that lock for our digital world.

## Key Points
- Cybersecurity protects systems, networks, devices, and data.
- The number of cyber attacks continues to increase as technology grows.
- Individuals as well as organizations require cybersecurity.
- The main objective is to prevent unauthorized access and protect sensitive information.
